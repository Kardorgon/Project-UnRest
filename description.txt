Project Unrest description:
Infinite roguelike/DnD type game

walkthrough of current game:
move using: WSAD
point and click on the items on the ground and on the enemies to interact
open inventory using I
click on an item in the inventory to equip it/consume it
Attacks are made using dice rolls. Each 3+ roll gives a hit that is countered by Enemies armour. If no.attacks > enemy armor = hit
